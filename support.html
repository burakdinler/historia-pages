Historia: Civilization - Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I advance to the next age?

Research the 4th Science node (science_4) of your current age. This will unlock the next age. You also need to meet the minimum resource and building requirements shown in the age transition screen.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the Gem Store in the app and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. This will restore your Premium Pass and any previous purchases.

My game progress was lost. What can I do?

Game progress is saved locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, your progress will be lost. We recommend keeping the app installed. A cloud save feature may be added in future updates.

How does the Premium Pass work?

The Premium Pass is a monthly subscription that removes all ads, doubles your offline earnings, and provides exclusive bonuses. You can cancel anytime through your Apple ID subscription settings.

How does the Prestige system work?

Once you reach a certain point in the game, you can "prestige" to reset your progress in exchange for permanent bonuses. These bonuses make your next playthrough faster and more powerful.

What are gems used for?

Gems can be used to speed up research, boost production, and purchase special items in the Gem Store. You can earn gems by watching rewarded video ads or purchasing gem packs.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Historia: Civilization > Cancel Subscription. Your premium benefits will continue until the end of the current billing period.
