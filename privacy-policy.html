Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Burak Dinler ("we", "us", "our") operates the Historia: Civilization mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Identifier (IDFA): If you grant tracking permission, we collect your Identifier for Advertisers to serve personalized ads through Google AdMob.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous gameplay data such as game progress, session duration, and feature usage.
  • Purchase History: Records of in-app purchases made within the App.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Photos or media

2. How We Use Information

  • To display advertisements (Google AdMob)
  • To process in-app purchases (Apple StoreKit)
  • To save your game progress locally on your device
  • To improve the App experience

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases. Apple Privacy Policy

4. Ad Tracking (ATT)

The App requests your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before collecting your IDFA for personalized advertising. You can deny this permission and still use the App with non-personalized ads. Premium Pass subscribers see no ads.

5. Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. We do not transmit your game data to external servers. No account creation is required.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App does not require any personal information to use.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Deny ad tracking permission at any time via iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking
  • Delete all game data by uninstalling the App
  • Restore purchases via the App's settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
